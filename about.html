About Us

KM GLOBAL LOGISTICS CO.,LTD

KM Global Logistics is a young company founded with the mission to deliver flexible, reliable, and cost-efficient logistics solutions for both domestic and international clients.

We are led by a founding and management team with over 10 years of hands-on experience in logistics, freight forwarding, and supply chain management. We not only understand the market but also the challenges that SMEs face in reality — enabling us to offer highly practical and tailored solutions.

For us, values like dedication – flexibility – integrity are not just words. They are our day-to-day commitment to building lasting relationships with our clients.

#Vision

Our Vision

To become a trusted logistics partner in Vietnam, offering high-quality services at reasonable costs.

#Mission

Our Mission

To accompany our clients on every shipment, delivering peace of mind through services that are on-time, transparent, and dedicated.
