About Us

KM GLOBAL LOGISTICS CO.,LTD

KM Global Logistics Co., Ltd is a company operating in the field of logistics and freight forwarding, specializing in providing comprehensive transportation solutions and optimizing supply chains for both domestic and international clients.

We were founded with the mission: "Your shipment, Our mission", committed to accompanying our valued customers on every step of the logistics journey — from the initial stage to the safe delivery of goods at their final destination.

Backed by a founding team with over 10 years of industry experience and a strong network of transport partners, KM Global Logistics confidently delivers services that are fast – accurate – flexible – cost-effective, meeting even the most demanding requirements of the modern logistics industry. We operate under the guiding principle:

"Reputation is our foundation – Quality is our commitment – Customers are our priority."
#Vision

Our Vision

To become a top-tier private logistics enterprise in Vietnam, taking the lead in offering flexible, secure, and optimized shipping solutions for a diverse client base — from individuals to multinational corporations — while maintaining exceptional service quality at competitive pricing.
