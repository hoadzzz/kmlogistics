#Services

Our Services

Sea Freight (FCL & LCL)

Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than-Container Load (LCL) services. Strong focus on USA & CANADA trade lanes. Full support from port of origin to port of destination (door-to-door if required).

Air Freight (Air & Express)

Fast and flexible shipping options based on your needs. Express international delivery service. Real-time cargo tracking with reliable schedules.

Inland Transportation

Nationwide delivery using trucks, containers, and specialized vehicles. Seamless connection to industrial zones and key cities. Constant shipment status updates.

Customs Brokerage

Customs declaration for import and export shipments. Document preparation, inspection handling, HS code classification. Fully compliant, time-saving, and tax-optimized solutions

Total Logistics – Integrated Solutions

Consolidation of transport – warehousing – distribution – customs. Streamlined and optimized supply chain operations. One-stop service – you hand us the goods, we handle the rest
