#Our Services

KM GLOBAL LOGISTICS

At KM Global Logistics, we provide a comprehensive range of logistics services designed to help clients save time, optimize costs, and operate efficiently — tailored for both individuals and businesses.

OCEAN FREIGHT (FCL & LCL) DOMESTIC & INTERNATIONAL

Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) Freight Forwarding.

We specialize in key trade lanes to the USA and Canada, offering reliable solutions for both FCL and LCL shipments.

Comprehensive support is provided from port of origin to port of destination — with door-todoor services available upon request

AIR FREIGHT & EXPRESS DELIVERY DOMESTIC & INTERNATIONAL

Fast & Flexible Transportation Tailored to Customer Needs.

We offer international express delivery services, ensuring rapid and reliable shipping to meet urgent demands.

Real-time shipment tracking is available to guarantee transparency and on-time delivery throughout the entire logistics process.

Inland Transportation

Domestic freight by truck, container, and specialized vehicles.

Door-to-door service with loading, unloading, labeling, and handling.

Regular shipment status updates provided.

CUSTOMS BROKERAGE SERVICES

Customs declaration for import and export shipments.

Support with documentation, inspection procedures, and HS code classification.

Compliance-driven solutions that reduce costs.

TOTAL LOGISTICS – COMPLETE & INTEGRATED SOLUTIONS

Integrated services: transportation – warehousing – forwarding – customs clearance.

End-to-end supply chain optimization.

One-stop solution – you deliver the goods, we handle the rest.
